The Governance Evaluation is split into 9 modules. All 9 modules can be completed at once taking 20-30 minutes or individual modules can be completed separately dependent on your time availability.

STATUS

Tells you where you are up to in completing the governance evaluation modules.

NOT STARTED

Click on start now to commence the module.

RESUME

Click on resume if you are returning to the module.

COMPLETED/DATE

When you have completed the module Completed and a date will be displayed.

If you wish to change your answers you can click on RESTART to redo the module questions
