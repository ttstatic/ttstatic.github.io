Governance Evaluation and Development Tool

MODULE 1: STRATEGIC DIRECTION
Formulation

Q1

Does the organisation have a current strategic plan that is relevant to the present environment, and which contains a set of objective and related strategies to drive the organisation's long-term performance?
Yes, there is a current, strategic plan with objectives and related strategies that are appropriate to the current environtment and will drive the organisation's long-term performance.
Yes, we have a current plan, but is either lacking in relevance to the present environtment, or the resulting strategies for driving the organisation's long-term performance are not appropriate.
No, we do not have a current strategic plan /unsure/not-applicable.
Oversight

Q2

Does the board effectively oversee the implementation of the current strategic plan?
Yes, the board's oversight role for strategy is achieved through regular monitoring of the key performance indicators and associated risks to determine the extent to which the strategies are being effectively pursued.
Yes, the board is engaged in an oversight role for the current strategy but it is not as effective as it could be due to irregular monitoring and/or lack of identification of relevant key performance measures, or risk.
No/unsure/not applicable

Q3

Does the board monitor the progress of the strategy through a yearly operational/business plan?
Yes, the board monitors progress against strategy through a yearly operational/business plan and budget, which has been created by management, with clear performance indicators, risks and an agreed reporting structure.
Yes, the board monitors progress of the strategy through its oversight role of a yearly operational/business plan, which has been created by management, but there may or may not be an agreed budget, clear performance indicators or an agreed reporting structure.
No/unsure/not applicable
Insight

Q4

Does the board periodically engage in opportunities to broaden and challenge its strategic thinking?
Yes, the board engages in regular opportunities to inspire, challenge and broaden its strategic thinking through regular 'insight' time.
Yes, the board endeavours to challenge and broaden its strategic thinking when the opportunity arises, but does not have regular 'insight' time set aside.
No/unsure/not applicable
