Governance verses Management Roles

Questions

Does the board understand and respect the difference between their governance roles and that of management?


Recommendation

Oversight of the quality of service that are provided by the organisation is a core responsibility of the board. The board should:

  • receive regular updates about the organisation's key quality indicators.
