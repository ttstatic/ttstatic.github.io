Formulation

It is recommended that a board periodically undertake a process to review its strategic plan.

  • analysis of current government and other relevant policy direction.
  • analysis of the organisation and recommendations for a sustainable future.

info Question 1: Formulation

Does the organisation have a current strategic plan that is relevant to the present environment, and which contains a set of objectives and related strategies to drive the organisation's long-term performance?

Yes, there is a current strategic plan with objectives and related strategies that are appropriate to the current environment.
Yes, we have a current plan, but it is either lacking in relevance to the present environment or the resulting strategies.
No, we do not have a current strategic plan.
