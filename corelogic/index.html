I need help choosing my package

Firstly, pick your industry group so we can provide a package most suitable for your business requirements.

Secondly, expand the View Included Features area by clicking on the row and it will expand to show which features are included in each package by default.

Use the dropdowns for Users and Geographic Access to customise the package where required.

If you’re still unsure and would like to speak to one of our sales consultants to talk you through the options available, please fill in the  Contact form and someone will be in touch with you shortly. Start over.

Government and Enterprise Plans

We have a dedicated account management team who look after our Government and Enterprise customers. If you are part of a Government organisation or have an Enterprise agreement or account with CoreLogic, please get in touch with our Customer Care team via email or call 1300 734 318 and they’ll direct you to the right team.

I am an existing customer

Our online signup portal is for new CoreLogic customers only, and cannot be used to update an account.

If you are an existing RP Data Professional or Cityscope/LED customer and wish to change your package in any way, our dedicated client relationship managers and growth coaching team will be happy to talk through what you’re looking to achieve. Please get in touch with Customer Care team via email or call 1300 734 318 and they’ll direct you to the right person to look after you.
