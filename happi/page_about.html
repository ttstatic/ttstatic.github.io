About Us


Happi is a community insight and interaction platform that enables people to help people in a fun, free, fast and easy way. Happi users can be get deals, win prizes and drive additional funding to a group or cause they care about all for simply responding to 5 question surveys. The more people participate, the more chances to win they get and the more funding their cause receives. All funding comes from Happi, not from the individuals and not from governments.

Happi works with charities, leagues, teams, student activity groups, volunteer groups, social enterprises, associations, chambers, unions and company HR departments to increase the survey responder base as well as the number of good causes we fund.

Happi enables merchants to advertise freely on Happi. We purchase all of the merchandise we give away to our members directly from these merchants. Happi users can use the coupons they download from Happi to make purchases directly from the merchants, who service and collect payment directly from the user. We do not delay collections or charge any commissions. The only fee to the merchant is a donation made to the group or cause the customer supports. Happi does not profit from the purchases we enable.

Happi enables our sponsors, which are brands, market researchers, media, and possibly our merchants and partners, to understand, engage and carry on 1 to 1 conversations at scale with people granularly defined profiles. To the user, it is just a 5 question survey that takes 30 seconds. To the sponsor, it is a cause-driven discussion with people whose profile, stated opinions or behaviour qualify them for a question, promotion or invitation. Happi aims to offer greater speed, precision, efficiency, and intimacy than our sponsors can enjoy on any other insight or interaction platform.

Our vision statement is to become the adwords of surveys and the world bank for community groups and causes globally. We believe this model provides powerful and unique benefits to our partners, members, suppliers and sponsors.
