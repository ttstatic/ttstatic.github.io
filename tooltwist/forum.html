What is ToolTwist Forum?

A plugin created by Tooltwist that can be embedded to your websites that provides collaboration features for your users.

Features

What do you and your visitors and subscribers get?


Join by signing-up
Your visitors and subscribers can join easily by signing-up themselves. They can also upload their own avatar/userpic.

Organise Topics
Organise topics by creating categories or rooms. Assists on managing topics for your collaboration.

Responsive design
Can be accessed from any device, be it smartphone, tablet, or desktop.

Vote up or vote down topics
By voting up or down a post, collaboration is organized and posts are easily found.

Tag users, topics or categories
Got a new or old posts you want to share? Tag your friends and other users.

Post URL (web page/video)
Share about a web page or a video that is related to the postâ€™s topic.

Notifications
Get a notification when someone replies to your post or mentions you. Never miss out on any posts, replies and comment by other users. Now you can instantly see them on your notification wall.

Other Features


Layout and Styles
You can change how your account looks by choosing from the multiple themes provided, or use your own CSS.

Calendar events
Never missed out on important dates. Post important events using the calendar widget. Remind other users about certain events and make it public or private or tag specific people or group of people.

Multi-lingual
Are your users from different parts of the world? Our multilingual feature will excite you!

Roles & Responsibilities
Different user roles (Admin, User, Moderator). Any abusive content can be reported by the users.
