What is ToolTwist Designer?

ToolTwist Designer is a sophisticated CMS that is used by marketing and user departments to design and maintain content. With ToolTwist Designer, you don't need to hire people with HTML, CSS or other web technology specialization knowledge. Pages can be laid out easily by simply dragging widgets onto the page. Changes and additions to your website can be done quickly by the people who conceive those changes. In most cases, there's no need to get an IT guy or web design company involved.
