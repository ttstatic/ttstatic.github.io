Education Solutions

Customized Portals that provides prospect students with quick and intuitive access to study courses via course search, course compare and apply functionality. Aids students to view their schedules, enrollments, attendance, assessments, financials, correspondence and notification. Students and prospects are allowed to do online enrollment and payment. It will also allow parents to have access and monitor the progress of the students, while providing users with a seamless interface and experience.

Features


Multi-lingual capability.

Can be implemented in multi-tenant setup.

Office 365 Integration.

SSO functionality.

Integrated to most commonly used payment gateways. (GOPI, KCP, AsiaPay, ChasePayment, Paypal, HSBC).

Performance scalability.

Highly Maintainable.
