About Us


The Baby Industry is a new and exciting baby product directory and community where you can find an extensive range of baby products available in Australia and everything you need to know about them. No more searching for hours on hundreds of sites. We are a directory building you a one stop website to find everything you need for baby.

As a community site it also allows parents to communicate with each other about the products and assist with product selections.

With exceptional knowledge in the Nursery Industry spanning over 15 years specialising in baby retail, marketing and online, we are excited to bring you an extensive range of baby products and detailed information about each product to assist you in finding what you are looking for from products listed on www.thebabyindustry.com

There will be many exciting features to assist you in making the best product choices from all products that are available on www.thebabyindustry.com. We can’t wait to launch this exciting website and bring you Australia’s baby product range like you have never seen before.
