Usage

1. On the list click on the download icon(colored green) of the desired template type. This will open a new tab or window.

2. In your browser menu, go to Edit, and then “Select All” or Ctrl+A ( or Command + A if you are on a Mac) to select everything that is displayed in the browser. This is the magic step that stores all the HTML onto your clipboard – so make sure you get this one right!

3. Paste (CTRL + V or Command +V) the html to the Gmail or Yahoo Mail message. Then you are done! This should work like a charm - and you should be able to see the HTML message directly in your message before you send it.

Gmail Canned Responses

These next steps will just make your life a little bit more easier. Its not a new one but just a quick tweak on your setting that will keep you from doing the previous steps again and again.

1. Hit that Gear icon and click on "Settings"

2. Click on the "Labs" tab, scroll down and look for "Canned Responses" (use the search field to find it if your index finger is stiff) and make sure it is Enabled.

3. Repeat the three steps(Now you will only run through these steps ones).

4. Now at the bottom right corner of the message box click on the icon next to delete icon then select "Canned responses" > "New canned response...".

5. Provide a canned response name.

6. Now that the template is saved onto your Canned Responses try composing a new message.

Result

Its that easy! You now have a built in template!
