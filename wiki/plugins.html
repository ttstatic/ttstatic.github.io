Materialize

This plugin transforms a simple form element to have a material design UI.

Usage

Download the "tt_materialize.js" and place it before your ending <body> tag.

Also download the "tt_materialize.css" and place it inside your <head> tag.


Initialize

$(inputSelector).materialize()


Adding a custom Placeholder

<input type="text"data-placeholder="Placeholder text...">
