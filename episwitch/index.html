Why EpiSwitchTM Portal?

EpiSwitch Portal offers easy access and expansion of the customer base for academic research and industry for first evidence and initial designs of data deconvolution and concordance in modalities of their choice against 3D genome architecture, as annotated by EpiSwitch.

It helps you to build early evidence and a validated path of development for patient stratification solution, disease understanding, new target identification.

It also offers valuable addition to informative designs to teams working in research, preclinical, translational medicine and clinical drug development teams.

Why EpiSwitchTM is Important?

It is the only technology that offers today high resolution annotations for regulatory 3D genome architecture and and robust industry standard sensitive detection through monitoring of conditional disseminating chromosome conformations

Chromosome conformations of the regulatory 3D genome architecture are recognized today as the key building blocks, which define differences in patient phenotypes and integrate various molecular regulatory modalities: genetic variants, transcriptional changes, metabolic, histone modifications, etc.
